    Clock Frame

    An unused clock can actually turn into something meaningful! Due to the innards of the clock is made up of different mechanical parts, it’s not possible to just simply recycle it. The frame of a clock is just like a picture frame, so I have decided to take out all the innards of the clock and can turn it to an actual picture frame.
