flip flop flat

Created by Michelle Carpentier

Flip flops would usually wear out in one summer, but the base of the unwanted flip flop can actually be turned into the base of a pair of flats of flippers to be worn at home. They are easy to make and would last for another period of time.


Supplies:
A cotton or nylon piece of clothe
Thicker thread for stitching or glue or one ended pin
Unwanted flip flop


Procedure:
1. Break off top support of unwanted flip flop
2. Clean base of flip flop
3. Cut the clothe into suitable top and side support for slipper or flat
4. Sometimes the base can be too hard or thick to stitch, so maybe using glue will be ideal in some cases
5. Attach all parts together and make sure parts are firmly attached
6. Enjoy your new flat and slipper!
