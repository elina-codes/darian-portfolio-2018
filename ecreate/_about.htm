about us

"Diverting waste is the ultimate act of sustainability."

We are a group of inviduals who want to achieve one common goal: to create a website that will help educate people on recycling, while at the same time open their minds to a different way of environmental sustainability. We want to allow them to transform something old, that might not work, into something new, different, fun, and possibly functional.

In doing this, they are going to be recycling their item, which can be considered as doing their part for the environment. Everyone can use this revelation as their motivation to continue to use our website and recycle through it.
