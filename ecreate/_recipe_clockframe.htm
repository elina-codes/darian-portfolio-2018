clock frame

Created by Stephanie Law

An unused clock can actually turn into something meaningful! Due to the innards of the clock is made up of different mechanical parts, itâ€™s not possible to just simply recycle it. The frame of a clock is just like a picture frame, so I have decided to take out all the innards of the clock and can turn it to an actual picture frame.


Supplies:
Clock (preferably with glass cover)
Pair of scissors
Ascrewdriver or similar tool
Small piece of poster paper
Glue or tape
A picture


Procedure:
1. Remove all mechanical parts of the clock from the back.
2. Remove the back layer of the clock.
3. Place the poster paper over the back layer of clock and trace the edges.
4. Use scissors to cut out the edges.
5. Glue the poster paper to the back layer of clock.
6. Organize the way you want to position the photos
7. Tape the photos onto the poster paper.
8. Place the back layer back into the clock frame.
9. Recycle all unused parts and leftover poster paper.
