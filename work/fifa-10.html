Overview

This was my first professional job in the industry. I was hired on by Electronic Arts as a co-op student on the design team and was kept on after I finished University. The main skills I learned on this project were how to rapidly iterate on wireframes, and how to properly communicate my designs.

Outcome

I was never updated on what our KPIs were on this project, so I don't really have a measurable goal. I did, however, gain a lot of new knowledge, some professional experience, and a start to my career.
