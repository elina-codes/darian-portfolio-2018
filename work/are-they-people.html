Are They People

Personal Project

A friend and I wanted to test out how adverts worked so we decided to make this website where you can rate a picture of an animal and decide if they think they are people or not. Once you vote, it loads a new image and you vote again. One boring weekend, we made it.

The backend of the site is down, but you can still interact with the front end prototype here .

(it might act weird if you have ad blockers enabled)

Designs

I was in charge of the functionality and recruited a friend to turn my wireframes into final mockups. Once I was happy with the mockups, I then created the entire front end of the website. My project partner was in charge of all of the backend programming.
