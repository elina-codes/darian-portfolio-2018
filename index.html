What I do

Set Goals

After the strategy and goals are defined, I conduct user research through various quantitative and qualitative methods, tailored to each project, to ensure a deep understanding of your target audience.

Adapt the Process

With a new understanding of your users, I then design for your target audience to get them from point A to point B as intuitively as possible, while hitting your KPIs.

UX it All

I then test my designs through various methods, reiterating where necessary, then work with my team to produce it while keeping them happy and engaged.
